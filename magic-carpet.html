Help Fund Replacement of Carpet for Robert Ferguson Observatory

Thank you for your interest in
funding the replacement of carpet throughout the Robert Ferguson Observatory!


No, it wasn't a magic carpet sighting at RFO (or the carpet of stars up in the night sky) that got people's attention; it was the unsightly carpet inside the observatory. The existing carpet is in sorry shape after many years of active use, a great testament to the thousands of visitors the RFO has received each year. We need to raise funds to replace the carpet in the classroom and telescope rooms. The estimate to replace the carpet is $8,000.
Can you help us replace the old carpet at the observatory?
